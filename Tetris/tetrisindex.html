Tetris

    - How to Play -
  • Use the tile pieces to fill up a row.
  • Rows that fill up will clear and earn 10 points.
  • Press "W" to rotate piece.
  • Press "A" and "D" to move left and right.
  • Press Down or "S" to drop faster.
